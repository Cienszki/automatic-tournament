How to Play Fantasy League

Team Building

  • • Select one player for each role (Carry, Mid, Offlane, Soft Support, Hard Support)
  • • Your total team MMR cannot exceed 25,000
  • • Choose wisely - higher MMR players cost more but may score more points
  • • You can change your lineup before each round starts

Scoring System

  • • Balanced Algorithm: All roles now competitive (93-115 PPG range)
  • • Role-Specific: Mid/Offlane buffed, Hard Support healing nerfed
  • • Excellence Bonuses: Multi-stat performances get massive rewards
  • • Duration Normalized: Fair scoring regardless of game length
